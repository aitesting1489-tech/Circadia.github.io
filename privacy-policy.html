  • Third-Party Integrations: We may receive information from third-party services you connect to your account, including payment processors, email providers, and analytics services.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following types of cookies on the Service:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Service to function properly, such as authentication and session management cookies.
  • Performance Cookies: Used to measure and improve the performance of the Service, including analytics cookies from Google Analytics.
  • Functional Cookies: Used to remember your preferences and customize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track your activity across websites and deliver targeted advertisements.

6.2 Third-Party Cookies

Third parties, including Google Analytics, may place cookies on your device to track your activities. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about how users interact with our Service and to measure website performance.

6.3 Cookie Control

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of the Service. For more information about managing cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

6.4 Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. Our Service does not currently respond to Do Not Track signals; however, you can use other tools to control cookies and tracking as described above.

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or other territories governed by GDPR, you have the following rights:

7.5 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to exceptions).
  • Right to Opt-Out: Direct us not to sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Limit Use: Limit our use of sensitive personal information to purposes necessary to provide the services you requested.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request at the address in Section 11. We will verify your identity before processing your request.
