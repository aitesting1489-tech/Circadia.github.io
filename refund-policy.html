Refund Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

At Circadia, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This refund policy outlines our commitment to customer satisfaction and the process for requesting refunds on payments made through our platform via Stripe.

Refund Eligibility

You may request a refund within the following conditions:

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the original purchase date
  • The transaction must have been completed through our Stripe payment system
  • You must provide a valid reason for the refund request
  • For digital products/services, refunds are available only if the product was not downloaded or used
  • For subscription services, refunds apply only to the current billing period

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are generally non-refundable:

  • Digital products that have been accessed or downloaded
  • Completed services that cannot be reversed
  • Purchases made with promotional codes or discounts (unless otherwise specified)
  • Refund requests submitted after 30 days from purchase
  • Transactions flagged for suspicious activity or fraud

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Circadia account
  2. Navigate to your order history or payment records
  3. Locate the transaction you wish to refund
  4. Click "Request Refund" and provide a detailed reason
  5. Submit the request for review

Alternatively, you can contact our support team at support@circadia.io with your transaction ID and refund reason.

Refund Processing

Processing Time: Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days. However, it may take up to 10-15 business days for the funds to appear in your original payment method, depending on your bank.

Once your refund request is submitted:

  • Our team will review your request (typically within 2-3 business days)
  • You will receive a notification via email regarding the status
  • If approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method
  • If declined, you will receive an explanation

Stripe Payment System

All refunds are processed through Stripe, our secure payment processor. Refunds are automatically credited back to the card or payment method you used at the time of purchase. Stripe ensures that refunds are handled securely and comply with payment industry standards.

For refunds initiated through Stripe, please allow:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days for the refund to appear
  • Digital Wallets: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 7-15 business days

Disputes and Chargebacks

If you file a chargeback or dispute with your bank before contacting us about a refund, it may complicate the refund process. We recommend using our refund request process first. If you have already initiated a chargeback, please inform our support team immediately.

Subscription Cancellations

If you cancel a subscription before the billing cycle completes:

  • Your subscription will be cancelled immediately
  • No refund will be issued for the current billing period
  • You will maintain access to your subscription benefits through the end of your current billing period
  • You will not be charged for future billing periods

Special Circumstances

Duplicate Charges

If you were charged twice for the same transaction, contact us immediately at support@circadia.io with proof of the duplicate charge. We will process a full refund for the duplicate transaction promptly.

Unauthorized Transactions

If you notice an unauthorized transaction on your account, contact Stripe directly through your bank or report it to us immediately. Do not delay, as this may affect your ability to dispute the charge.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our refund policy or need to request a refund, please contact us:

  • Email: support@circadia.io
  • Response Time: Within 24-48 hours

Please include your transaction ID, order date, and reason for the refund in your request.

Policy Updates

Circadia reserves the right to update this refund policy at any time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or by posting the updated policy on our website. Your continued use of our services after policy updates constitutes your acceptance of the new terms.
